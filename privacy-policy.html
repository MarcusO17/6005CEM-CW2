Privacy Policy

Welcome to the eDoc Hospital Appointment Booking System. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we are committed to protecting the personal information you provide. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we safeguard it in compliance with relevant privacy laws and standards.

The privacy policy was last updated on 10 November 2024.

When you use our appointment booking system, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information:

    Name, contact information (email, phone number), date of birth, NRIC, and other necessary details for appointment management.

  • Medical Information:

    Appointment history, doctors you have consulted, and any notes or information relevant to your appointment.

  • Technical Information:

    IP address, browser type, and usage data to improve the functionality and security of our system.

We use the information collected for:

  • Managing and scheduling appointments.
  • Sending appointment reminders and updates.
  • Ensuring the security and integrity of our system.
  • Improving our services and user experience.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Information sharing occurs only as required by law or to facilitate healthcare services.

We implement industry-standard security measure to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption:

    Data in transit is protected with TLS, and sensitive data-at-rest is encrypted.

  • Access Control:

    Only authorized personnel can access your data, based on their role in the healthcare process.

  • Regular Audits:

    We monitor and audit access logs to prevent authorized access.

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information and update it as needed.
  • Request deletion of your data when it is no longer needed for the purposes outlined.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable

By using our appointment booking system, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. Your continued use of the service signifies your acceptance of these terms.

If you have any questions or concerns about your data privacy, please contact us at support@edoc.com.
